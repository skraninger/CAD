# Parts for Bed Frame

## General Notes:

- Parts are all designed in OpenSCAD.
- Parameters can be changed as needed.
- Strength along layer lines are a main consideration

#### Main parameters to consider:

- Lengths
- Thicknesses
- Bolt / Screw holes

#### Three part types from simplest to most complex:

- Corner
- Swivel Clamp
- T Clamp

## Corner

- Three main shapes, Leg1, Leg2, Corner
- Printed at 45 degree angle to reduce supports and provide layer strength.
- Consider the thickness, leg, and inside dimensions.

## Swivel Clamp

- A pair clamps connected by an internal structure.
- Printed with no supports and layer lines supporting "clamping" dimension
- Consider thickness, inside dimensions, and overall part size.
- Parts could be created in a "glue" together design with possible carbon rod reinforcement
- Options for clamp tighteners (bolt head, screw inserts, cut threads, clip on)

## T Clamp

- Printed as two parts to be glued for clamping strength, or as a single part.
- Both designs have possible failure points when 3D printed

- Consider thickness, inside dimensions, and overall part size.
- Parts could be created in a "glue" together design with possible carbon rod reinforcement
- Options for clamp tighteners
- Material selection
